本实验中用到的配置命令如下：
1.	开启/关闭设备VLAN特性：vlan vlan_id
2.	查看VLAN视图：show vlan [id vlan-id]
3.	删除VLAN：no vlan vlan_id
4.	选择以太网接口并进入配置模式：interface gi [mod-num/port-num]
5.	查看指定以太网接口的配置信息：show interface [interfa-id][counters|description|status|switchport|trunk]
6.	配置多个以太网接口：interface range [port-range]
7.	向VLAN中添加/删除接口：switchport access vlan [vlan-id] no  switch access vlan
8.	设置接口的IP地址：ip address ip-address mask   no ip address
9.	更改二层接口模式：switchport mode {access|trunk} no switchport mode
10.	VLAN描述：description strina  undo description
